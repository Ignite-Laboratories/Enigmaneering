**Sub Byte Indexes** - A sub-byte index is any bit range less than 8 bits.

      Index | Name
        1   | Bit
        2   | Crumb
        3   | Note
        4   | Nibble
        5   | Flake
        6   | Morsel
        7   | Shred

**Super Byte Indexes** - A super-byte index is any bit range wider than 8 bits.  I almost _never_ refer to these, but
for posterity's sake I'd like to include them here -

      Index | Name
       10   | Run
       12   | Scale
       16   | Motif
       24   | Riff
       32   | Cadence
       48   | Hook
       64   | Melody
      128   | Verse
